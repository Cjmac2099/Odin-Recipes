BBQ Pizza Recipe

This recipe is based on a BBQ Chicken Pizza from Pizza Kitchen. However this recipe will be budget friendly and can be a great alternative for family dinner night instead of ordering takeout. This recipe should take around 3 hours to complete (including time to let chicken marinate in BBQ sauce)

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Barbeque Sauce
  • olive Oil
  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Red Onions
  • Cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Cut up chicken pieces and let marinate in BBQ sauce for about 2 hours. Let marinate longer if desired.
  2. When ready, preheat your oven. If you're using a pizza stone, then let this preheat for at least 30 minutes.
  3. While waiting for oven to heat, cook chicken in saute pan with some olive oil until it becomes nice and browned.
  4. Roll or stretch the dough until it becomes a nice circle
  5. Spread the remaining BBQ sauce on the crust until completely covered and smooth out any wrinkles. Make sure to leave space for the crust.
  6. Add cheese to crust as desired
  7. Add the chicken on top before adding desired toppings. Once added, add more cheese on top of the two.
  8. Bake the pizza until cheese is melted and crust is a nice browned color.
  9. Once finished, remove pizza from oven and add any other desired toppings. Let sit until cook. Once finished, feel free to dig in.
